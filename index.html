About

Jieqi Almanac is an offline iOS app that tracks the 24 traditional Chinese solar terms (jieqi 節氣) — a 2,000-year-old astronomical calendar system that divides the solar year into 15° arcs of the Sun's apparent ecliptic longitude.

Each term is computed to the second using the same astronomical algorithm published by Purple Mountain Observatory. The app includes 72 pentads (三候), curated classical poems, year almanac, and Home Screen / Lock Screen widgets.

Available in 6 languages: Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese. One-time purchase — no subscriptions, no ads, no data collection.

FAQ

Why does the app cost money?

Jieqi Almanac is a one-time purchase. We don't show ads, sell your data, or charge subscription fees. The price covers ongoing development, Apple Developer Program fees, and our commitment to keep the app fully offline and ad-free.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Jieqi Almanac does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app makes zero network requests — all calculations and content are bundled within the app itself. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How accurate are the solar term times?

Solar term times are computed to the second using the VSOP87 / Meeus astronomical algorithm, the same standard used by Purple Mountain Observatory. The app covers years 1900 through 2100. Typical accuracy is within a few seconds of authoritative ephemerides.

Why is the original Chinese text not translated?

Classical Chinese poetry and the names of solar terms carry meanings that often dissolve in translation. We preserve the original Chinese as a fixed display, with translations layered above as overlays — so you can appreciate both the original sound and the translated meaning.

Will Apple Watch / iPad / macOS be supported?

iPad is supported in version 1.0. Apple Watch and macOS support are under consideration based on demand.

My widget isn't updating. What should I do?

Widgets refresh based on iOS scheduling. If a widget seems stuck, try removing and re-adding it from your Home Screen. Make sure background app refresh is enabled in iOS Settings → Jieqi Almanac.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com within 14 days of purchase.
